How to Delete Your Information

You can delete all your personal information (name, mobile number, and role) collected by the Find Court Lahore app using any of the following methods:

  1. Option 1: Uninstall the app from your device.
  2. Option 2: Go to your device settings → Apps → Find Court Lahore → Storage → Clear Data.

Request Manual Deletion

If you want us to manually remove your Firebase data, please email us at contact2smartboy@gmail.com using the same phone number you entered in the app. Your data will be deleted within 7 working days of the request.
